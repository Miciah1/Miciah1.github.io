Privacy

User Privacy and Our Policies

The privacy policy here is very simple. Your advertising information and browsing practices will not be monetized. At no point in the future are there any plans to sell your browsing habit data. Your information is completely confidential here. The only possible scenario that would compell us to furnish user data to any entity would be a subpeona. However, even a subpeona may not meet our/my necessary criteria required for the provisions of aa users data. Enjoy yourself here. Digest content and provide constructive solutions in the form of criticism when comments are allowed.

Cookies

This website may use cookies at any time. We may use both general setting cookies as well as tracking and third party analytics cookies. Cookies will not be used to compile, sell or divuldge any user information. Third parties may sell user data. Third party divulgence is beyond our control. Check the page footer for tools that can help keep your data private. Tools such as TOR browser, Opera [built in VPN FREE], Mozilla with Container Safety Browsing are all usefull tools that help to keep your data safe.

Golden Rule

Respect the privacy policies of third party providers. The onus is on you to determine what third parties policies are. Above all respect another users privacy. Intimidation, Cruelty and harassment of any kind towards any user will not be tolerated. Opinions and criticism that is of a constructive nature is allowed, above all be chivalrous and good to all users.
